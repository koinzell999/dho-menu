About DHO

DHO is a dessert and drinks destination crafted for those who appreciate quality flavors and a welcoming atmosphere. From our signature waffles and crepes to handcrafted cocktails and fresh fruit salads, every item on our menu is made with care.

Our Story

Born from a passion for bringing people together over great food, DHO started as a small corner shop and has grown into a beloved neighborhood spot. We believe desserts should be an experience — not just a treat.

What We Offer

  • 🧇 Freshly made waffles with premium toppings
  • 🥞 Soft crepes with rich fillings
  • 🍨 Creamy ice cream and frozen desserts
  • 🍹 Handcrafted cocktails and fresh juices
  • 🍰 Homemade cakes and fruit salads
